Azerbaijan Energy Engineering and Consulting LLC (AEEC LLC - AEEC), which has been providing engineering and consulting, research services for nearly 30 years, proposes a new business model based on the needs of the modern era, using its experience in restructuring the company's work for the near future.
AEEC performs large-scope engineering & consulting (research) activities such as, power sector development program, design and implementation of the regulator, technical assistance on tariffs, social support for electricity, macroeconomic analysis on the evaluation of the power intensity of all industries; assessment of the impact of energy price changes to other sectors of economy; evaluation of the macroeconomic impact of the export-import potential of electricity and natural gas to Azerbaijan’s economy; research and study of potential of non-traditional (renewable) energy sources, modern green technology and energy efficiency; estimation of exploitation of hydrocarbon resources; consulting and research services on strategic development, management, and government/private sector interfaces for Azerbaijan’s fuel and energy complex; macroeconomic modeling for projecting demand for energy resources; system planning; development and improvement of commercial, metering, and grid codes etc.
AEEC has implemented many projects financed by number of international organizations and companies, such as World Bank, ADB, EBRD, USAID, BP, PA Consulting, Nexant and etc. on various projects related to fuel-energy sector.
